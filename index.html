Engineering Projects

DIY Brushless Quadcopter

Assembled and flight tested a brushless quadcopter from a DIY kit to gain hands-on experience with UAV systems, electronics, and aircraft integration.

In calm conditions, the drone flew smoothly and maintained stable control. During testing in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts of 10–15 mph, the aircraft drifted downwind and had difficulty holding position.

Skills: UAV assembly, wiring, troubleshooting, flight testing, and technical documentation

Status: Flight testing in progress

PLA Filament Environmental Monitor

Designed and built an Arduino-based humidity and temperature monitor to evaluate room conditions for PLA filament storage.

The system uses an Arduino Uno R3, DHT11 sensor, LCD1602 display, 10k potentiometer, and breadboard wiring. It displays live humidity, temperature, and a simple storage condition status.

Skills: Arduino programming, sensor integration, LCD wiring, breadboard prototyping, and troubleshooting

Status: Functional prototype complete
