About Me

Hi, I'm Roberto Rivas, an aerospace engineering student with a strong interest in space, aviation, and the technology that makes exploration possible. I've always enjoyed understanding how things work, and engineering gives me a way to turn that curiosity into something useful.

I learn best through hands-on projects because building, testing, and improving something teaches me more than just reading about it. Each project helps me become more confident with engineering, problem-solving, and working through mistakes.

My long-term goal is to help advance aerospace technology and contribute to the future of human space exploration.
