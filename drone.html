Project Overview

This project involved assembling and flight testing a brushless quadcopter from a DIY kit to gain hands-on experience with UAV systems, electronics, aircraft integration, and flight testing.

Building the aircraft helped me better understand how the frame, brushless motors, ESCs, flight controller, battery, and power distribution system work together as one complete aircraft.

Objective

Gain practical experience building and operating a quadcopter while improving my understanding of UAV systems and real-world flight performance.

Flight Testing

In calm weather, the quadcopter flew smoothly and maintained stable control throughout the flight.

During another flight in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts between 10 and 15 mph, the aircraft drifted in the direction of the wind and struggled to maintain its position.

This demonstrated how environmental conditions can significantly affect lightweight UAV performance.
