Project Overview

I assembled and flight tested a brushless quadcopter to gain hands-on experience with UAV systems, electronics, aircraft integration, and flight testing.

Building the aircraft helped me better understand brushless motors, ESCs, the flight controller, battery systems, wiring, and how all of the components work together.

Objective

Build and test a quadcopter while developing practical experience with UAV hardware, assembly, troubleshooting, and flight performance.

Flight Testing

During testing in calm conditions, the quadcopter flew smoothly and maintained stable control.

A second flight was conducted in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts between 10 and 15 mph. Under these conditions the aircraft drifted with the wind and struggled to hold its position.
