Project Overview

Assembled and flight tested a brushless quadcopter from a DIY kit to gain hands-on experience with UAV systems, electronics, aircraft integration, and flight performance.

The project helped me better understand how the frame, brushless motors, flight controller, wiring, and power system work together as one aircraft system.

Flight Testing

In calm conditions, the drone flew smoothly and maintained stable control.

During another test in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts of 10–15 mph, the aircraft had difficulty holding position and drifted in the direction of the wind.

This showed how strongly wind can affect a lightweight UAV and why testing under different environmental conditions is important.
