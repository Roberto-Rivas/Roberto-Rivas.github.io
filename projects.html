Brushless Quadcopter Flight Test Platform

Assembled and flight tested a brushless quadcopter from a DIY kit to gain experience with UAV systems, motors, electronics, aircraft integration, and flight performance.

In calm conditions, the drone flew smoothly and maintained stable control. During testing in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts of 10–15 mph, the aircraft drifted with the wind and was unable to consistently hold position.

Skills: UAV assembly, wiring, troubleshooting, flight testing, observation, and technical documentation.
