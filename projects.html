Project Portfolio

These projects document my work in aerospace, embedded systems, electronics, flight testing, and technical problem-solving.

DIY Brushless Quadcopter

Assembled and flight tested a brushless quadcopter from a DIY kit to gain experience with UAV systems, motors, electronics, aircraft integration, and flight performance.

In calm conditions, the drone flew smoothly and maintained stable control. During testing in approximately 8 mph winds with gusts of 10–15 mph, the aircraft had difficulty holding position and drifted downwind.

Skills: UAV assembly, wiring, troubleshooting, flight testing, observation, and technical documentation

Status: Flight testing in progress

PLA Filament Environmental Monitor

Designed and built an Arduino-based humidity and temperature monitor to evaluate room conditions for storing PLA filament.

The system uses an Arduino Uno R3, DHT11 sensor, LCD1602 display, 10k potentiometer, and breadboard circuit. It displays live room humidity and temperature.

Skills: Arduino programming, sensor integration, LCD wiring, breadboard prototyping, and troubleshooting

Status: Functional prototype complete

Model Rocket Flight Testing

Built and launched a model rocket while documenting weather, flight behavior, parachute deployment, estimated altitude, and total flight time.

Skills: Rocket assembly, launch preparation, flight observation, data recording, and performance analysis

Status: Testing and documentation in progress
